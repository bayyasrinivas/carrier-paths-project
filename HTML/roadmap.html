Road Maps

A road map provides a step-by-step guide outlining the essential skills, knowledge, and milestones needed to achieve a specific goal or career path, offering a clear direction for students and professionals.

Computer Science Engineering (CSE)

Year 1-2: Foundation

  • Programming Languages (C, C++, Java, Python)
  • Data Structures & Algorithms
  • Computer Organization and Architecture
  • Discrete Mathematics
  • Operating Systems
  • Database Management Systems (DBMS)
  • Web Development Basics

Year 3: Core Subjects

  • Software Engineering
  • Computer Networks
  • Design and Analysis of Algorithms
  • Theory of Computation
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning Basics
  • Cybersecurity Fundamentals

Year 4: Specialization & Projects

  • Electives (e.g., Cloud Computing, Data Science, Blockchain)
  • Capstone Project
  • Internship or Industry Training
